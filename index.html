About

Anass Marzouki is an Assistant Professor at the International University of Rabat (UIR) with a Ph.D. in Geosciences & Natural Resources and a Master's in Geosciences and Mineral Resources. Combining deep domain knowledge with advanced technical skills, he specializes in geomatics and web mapping ‚Äî transforming complex geoscience data into clear, usable maps and tools for research, teaching, and applied projects.
